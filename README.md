## About Me

<img src="profile.png" alt="Anna G. Morgani" width="200" style="border-radius: 50%;"/>

Hi, I'm **Anna Morgani (Devlen)**!

I'm a recent graduate of UC Berkeley's **Master of Science in Molecular Science & Software Engineering** program with a passion for intergrating complex biology and scalable software architecture. During my graduate studies, I focused on building my knowledge of bioinformatics methods, software engineering best practices, and machine learning algorithms through industry-sponsored and academic projects.

Previously, I've worked as a research scientist in the immuno-oncology and neurodegenerative disease spaces, focusing on drug mechanism-of-action (MOA) and structure-activity-relationship (SAR) studies. I love bridging my knowledge of biology and my programming skills to build tools that make scientists' lives easier. In other words, I build easy-to-use, reproducible, and well-documented data analysis workflows to accelerate the drug development process. 

In my free time, you can find me hiking, exploring new restaurants, or planning my next National Park trip.
